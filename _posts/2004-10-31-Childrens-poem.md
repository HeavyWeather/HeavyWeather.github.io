Gangly Men get everywhere...
Curtain rails, cat's entrails,
Even in balls of rotten old hair.
Push in tight,
No need for the light,
Just sit and stare,
With pale second sight.
Wrapped up small,
No shadow at all...
Gangly Men unfurl in the walls.
